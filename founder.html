Lehman Blackstone Launchpad

Learn the different approaches such as traditional, social, or impact entrepreneurship. Learn how to identify your opportunities and test your concepts. If you’re an undergraduate or graduate student, apply be part of a Lehman College’s Blackstone LaunchPad Cohort!

A total of 20 students will are selected every to participate in a three-month-long Cohort. At the end of the semester, you will be prepared for the Blackstone LaunchPad Pitch Day at Lehman College. As a member of a Cohort you will:

  • Learn entrepreneurial skills to run a start-up
  • Receive 3 months of entrepreneurial training from the Young Ambitious Ones (YAO)
  • Connect with successful entrepreneurs
  • Earn a chance to pitch your idea locally and nationally for cash prizes
